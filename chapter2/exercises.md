# Chapter 2 Exercises


## Question 1: Laundry pipelinig quiz
Suppose we have three stages when it comes to laundry i.e. washer, dryer, folder. Each one of them takes 1 hour to complete and we have 10 loads of laundry then: <br> 
A) what's the latency without pipelining?___________<br>
B) what's the latency with pipelining?__________<br>
ANSWER: A-> 30 hours, B-> 3 + 9 = 12 hours because first load takes 3 hours and then the remaining loads take 1 hour each <br>

## Question 2: Instruction pipelining quiz
Suppose we have 5 stage pipelined processor, each stage takes 1 clock cycle to complete and we have 10 instructions in our program: <br> 
A) what's the latency without pipelining?__________ <br>
B) what's the latency with pipelining?_____________<br>
ANSWER: A-> 50 cycles, B-> 5 + 9 = 14 cycles <br>
